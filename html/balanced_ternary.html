Use this calculator to gain a deeper understanding of the beauty of balanced ternary.

The "Λ" character represents positive one (+1), and the "V" character represents negative one (-1).

Below are the addition and multiplication tables for balanced ternary. Note how few "carries" are necessary, especially when compared to the base 10 which we are familiar with.
